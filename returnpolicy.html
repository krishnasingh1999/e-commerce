RETURN AND REPLACEMENT POLICY

Thanks for shopping at www.MamaMarts.com

If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Because of the nature of our goods (perishable food items) our policy lasts 2 days from the day of delivery (including it). If 2 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or replacement.

REPLACEMENT
  • You will have to initiate a replacement within 8 hours from the date you received it.
  • To be eligible for a replacement, you must share images of the product you have received.
  • Your item needs to have proof of purchase (order no or snapshot of the order from the app/website indicating the items and date of purchase).
  • Once we have confirmed our purchase, we will begin the process of replacement for the item you are not satisfied with.
  • Your replacement will reach you within 48 hours of the date of complain raised.

Refunds

Refunds can be requested if:
  1. Items are missing from your order.
  2. Quality is not as per your expectation.
  3. Order or item from the order is not delivered.

If your complain falls within the above 3 listed causes, we will refund the order amount or the item amount depending on the complaint.

Your order/item needs to have proof of purchase (order no or snapshot of the order from the app/website indicating the items and date of purchase).

Your refund will be initiated immediately once we have confirmed the purchase and established the cause of refund.

You can opt for MamaMarts Loyalty Points or choose to get the refund in your original source of payment.

We will immediately initiate the refunds from our end once we have established proof of purchase but each bank has a different duration to credit the refund amount. We would request you to check with your bank in case of any delays.

Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit/debit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank.There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us.
